What is CI/CD?

CI/CD stands for Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment. It's a method to frequently deliver apps to customers by introducing automation into the stages of app development. The main concepts attributed to CI/CD are continuous integration, continuous delivery, and continuous deployment.

Use Cases of CI/CD

  • Automating the build, test, and deployment process to reduce manual errors.
  • Ensuring consistent and repeatable processes for application deployment.
  • Speeding up the release of new features and bug fixes.
  • Providing faster feedback loops to developers.
  • Improving collaboration among development, testing, and operations teams.
